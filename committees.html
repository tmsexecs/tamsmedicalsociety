COMMITTEES

Click on the symbols to learn more about them!

TMS Shadows

An educational committee that provides students with shadowing opportunities and hosts Dinner With Doctors, connecting TAMS students directly with physicians across medical specialties.

Women in Medicine

An educational committee partnered with the TAMS Girl Up Chapter, striving for equality and providing a safe space for all interested in the medical field.

TAMS Alzheimer's Care

A volunteering committee dedicated to providing support and company for Alzheimer's patients.

TAMS Neuroscience Society

An educational committee dedicated to teaching and providing opportunities in the field of neuroscience.

Denton Nursing Home

A volunteering committee partnered with HOPE, dedicated to providing an opportunity for students to contribute positively to assisted living communities and interact with senior citizens.

Outreach

A volunteering committee dedicated to teaching students in Denton about the diverse field of medicine.

Nightingale

The TAMS chapter of Nightingale Advocacy, an organization dedicated to advocating for young voices in America in an attempt to bring health care policy changes.

Stethoscope Committee

Our committee dedicated to creating our annual Stethoscope journal — written, edited, illustrated, and designed entirely by TAMS students.

TAMS Red Cross

An educational committee dedicated to training the TAMS community in various medical activities, humanitarian aid, and serving with compassion.

TAMS Biomedical Engineering

An educational committee dedicated to creating a community of students passionate about biomedical engineering.

Best of Texas

A committee celebrating Texas culture, community, and service while giving TAMS students new ways to connect and get involved.

Healing With Music

A volunteering committee partnered with Dull Roar that enables groups of musicians to visit nursing homes and play music for residents.
