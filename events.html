EVENTS

Click on the event icons to learn more about them!

Committee Carnival

At the beginning of the year, TMS committees hold a mini field-day-esque showcase of their activities for the upcoming school year!

Dinner With Doctors

Hosted by the Shadows Committee, students have dinner with doctors in various medical fields, providing opportunities to learn and ask questions about the world of medicine!

Aid The Cause

Each November, we host a TAMS-wide talent show where students can watch their peers perform and donate money for medically-related cause -- all while enjoying good food and fun!

Stethoscope Launch Party

The hard work of the Stethoscope committee is shown during the Stethoscope launch party where publishers and editors come together to celebrate their work!

Shear Kindness

Partnering with Subaru, we hold a hair donation drive where hair is donated to Children With Hair Loss© and Matter of Trust©!

Spring Fling

Held by the newly-chosen board, we host a field day event where students get to wind down and celebrate the past year!
