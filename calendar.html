Specific dates are announced each school year via general assembly meetings and social media. Follow @tamsmedicalsociety for up-to-date announcements.
Fall Semester

🎡 Committee Carnival

TMS committees hold a mini field-day-esque showcase of their activities for the upcoming school year. New and returning students explore committees and sign up.

🍽️ Dinner With Doctors

Hosted by the Shadows Committee. Students dine with physicians from various specialties in an intimate, conversational setting.

🎤 Aid The Cause

TMS's signature TAMS-wide talent show and fundraiser. Students perform, donate, and enjoy food — all for a medically-related cause.

Spring Semester

📔 Stethoscope Launch Party

The annual Stethoscope Journal is released. Publishers, editors, writers, and designers celebrate their work together.

✂️ Shear Kindness

Hair donation drive partnered with Subaru. Hair donated to Children With Hair Loss© and Matter of Trust©.

🌸 Spring Fling

End-of-year field day hosted by the newly elected board. A celebration of the year's accomplishments and a send-off for graduating seniors.
