What Is TMS?

The TAMS Medical Society (TMS) is a student-run organization with the purpose of bridging the TAMS student body to the medical field. With our various fundraising events, committees, volunteer opportunities, and educational programs, TMS hopes to provide interested students with opportunities to connect with the healthcare field throughout the school year.
