Spring Fling

And to end of the year is Spring Fling! As their first event, the newly-chosen baby board (partnering with McConnell Hall Association) puts together a field-day event for the entirety of TAMS. The event is held after Rosecutting, TAMS' annual student life ceremony commemorating the student body throughout the year. With food, games, and MHA's senior bonfire to end the night, the field day is our way of saying thank you with this final hoorah as the year comes to a close.
